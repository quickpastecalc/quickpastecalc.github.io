Quick Paste & Calculate Online

Need to calculate a math expression quickly? Simply copy and paste it into our calculator for instant results. Perfect for on-the-go calculations!

    Available Math Operations

    + : Addition (e.g., 2 + 3)

    - : Subtraction (e.g., 5 - 1)

    * : Multiplication (e.g., 4 * 6)

    / : Division (e.g., 10 / 2)

    ** : Exponentiation (Power) (e.g., 2 ** 3 for $2^3$)

    You can use parentheses () to control the order of operations.

    Numbers in scientific E-notation (e.g., 1.2E3) are also supported.
